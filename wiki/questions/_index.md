# Questions

Filed answers to questions you've asked the wiki. Good answers get filed back here as new pages so they compound instead of disappearing into chat history.

## When to file an answer

- The answer required reading 3+ wiki pages to synthesize.
- The answer is something a future-you (or a colleague) would ask again.
- The answer surfaced new gaps or open questions.
- You explicitly say `file this answer` or `save this`.

## Conventions

- Filename matches the question (or a slugified version).
- Frontmatter `question:` carries the original verbatim wording.
- `answer_quality:` tracks maturity: `draft | solid | definitive`.
- Always include "What we still don't know" — questions surface other questions.
