# Concepts

Frameworks, models, ideas. A concept is anything that's *abstract enough to be reused* — a methodology, a mental model, a pattern, a principle.

## What goes here vs. elsewhere

- **Concept** = abstract, reusable (e.g. "Reciprocal Rank Fusion", "Forgetting Curve")
- **Entity** = concrete thing in the world (use `entities/`)
- **Reference** = quick lookup (use `references/`)
- **Comparison** = analysis of multiple things (use `comparisons/`)
