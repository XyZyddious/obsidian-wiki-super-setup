# Comparisons

Side-by-side analyses. Use when you need to weigh multiple things — frameworks, products, approaches — along shared dimensions.

## What goes here vs. elsewhere

- **Comparison** = side-by-side of 2+ things (e.g. "Wiki vs. RAG")
- **Concept** = a single framework or idea
- **Decision** = a choice you made (use `journal/` or `projects/<name>/`)

## Conventions

- Frontmatter `subjects:` lists what's being compared as wikilinks.
- `dimensions:` lists the comparison axes.
- `verdict:` is a one-line headline conclusion (it shows up in `summary:` retrieval).
- Always include a "When to choose which" section.
